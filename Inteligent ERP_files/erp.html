Inteligent ERP 20019

Backend Elaborated con c#, en microsfot Visual Studio, Fast Report, Front End Development Delphi 10.3 Consumer of JSON, Database in Mysql Server and SQL Server according to customer needs, 6 modules, manager for the Intelligent ERP administration system, whose objective is to focus on the opportunity to manage your projects online, plan income and expenses, expenses and supplies, you will have greater control of information and data by standardizing document formats originated by the processes and operation of all areas, and you will achieve the reduction of time by avoiding the double capture of information, since our system has communication between all the modules.

Warehouse Control

Functions

  • Prepare Requisitions, Purchase Orders
  • Prepare Quotes and Recommendation of best Suppliers
  • Prepare material traceability
  • Prepare inputs and outputs of materials
  • Prepare Control of Inventary, Administratión de many Warehouses

Administrative control

Functions

  • Prepares Income and Expense Control of the project company.
  • Produces Graphics of the Status of the same
  • Prepare Electronic Invoices.
  • Prepares Cash Flow Control and Banks.
  • Prepares Banking State Control.

Control of Humans Resources

Functions

  • Prepare Control de Kardex Electronic.
  • Prepares Personnel Requisitions, Resource Assignment Control.
  • Generate Contracts, Settlements and Guard Control.
  • Control of Employees, Assignment of Work Fronts by projects.
  • Prepares Credentials, Letter of Recommendation, Source of Information to Payroll Modules.

Sales Controls

Functions

  • Prepare Sales Quotes.
  • Elaborates Control and Monitoring of Clients.
  • Create a Customer Activity Log.
  • Create Salesperson Productivity Graphics.
  • Prepares Invoices Based on Quotes and Managerial Reports.

Control de Proyectos

Funciones

  • Prepare daily reports and photographic reports.
  • Issuance of validated reports with electronic signature.
  • Control of personnel and equipment logs
  • Control of actual and scheduled progress
  • Issuance of managerial reports.
  • Flow control by project

Precios Unitarios

Funciones

  • Prepare work budgets.
  • Prepares the scheduled administration of resources for the project.
  • Prepares Sales prices and Project Resource Costs.
  • Elaborates the scheduling of resources labor, equipment, tools, materials.
  • Link to Work Monitoring and Control modules, to compare Costs between programmed and actual.
  • Make Real Salary Factor Calculations.
